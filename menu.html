Types of Fish

Here are some of the most common types of fish you can keep in an aquarium:

  • Tetras

    Small, peaceful fish that come in a variety of colors.

  • Guppies

    Colorful, active fish that are easy to care for.

  • Angelfish

    Graceful, elegant fish that can grow up to 6 inches long.

  • Betta

    Colorful, solitary fish that can be aggressive towards other fish.

  • Corydoras

    Bottom-dwelling fish that are great at cleaning up leftover food.

  • Gourami

    Labyrinth fish that have a unique breathing organ and come in many colors.

  • Discus

    Colorful, social fish that can be difficult to care for but are worth the effort.

  • Goldfish

    Popular fish that come in many different varieties and can live for decades.
